Props for Peds

Props for Peds is a fundraiser launched by a 17 year old kid, Nicholas Gioules, who's been dedicated for most of his life to helping the disabled and lending care to those who need. Since a young age, he's been taught to treat people with the utmost respect and give his help to those who may be in more of a need for it. Helping young kids with disabilities or disorders has been a passion of his ever since a little kid, and to further aid to these children, he's created a fundraiser which has gave him the ability to do so.
