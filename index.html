Props4Peds

Props4Peds is a non-for-profit organization launched by two siblings, Nicholas and Mary Gioules, to provide opportunities and support to children with challenges, so they can pursue active lifestyles through physical fitness and competitive athletics. Nicholas & Mary believe that participation in sports at any level builds self-esteem, increases independence and enhances the quality of life of children. Helping young children with disabilities has been a passion of each of theirs ever since they were children.

Did you know children with special needs have many barriers in their ability to participate in sports?

These limitations include costs, programs available to them, and social support from their communities. Our mission is to help integrate children of all abilities into recreational and sports activities. Children with special needs have the right to participate and opportunities should be given to them! Props4Peds was created to do just that.

Mary Gioules

Co-President

As the co-founder of this organization, my brother and I share the same goal- to help children strengthen their minds and bodies while doing what they love. I have been an athlete all my life, and have always wanted to use my skills to help the children who I grew up around to be their best selves. I also have a passion to bring the sport I love (lacrosse) to young girls from various communities that do not have easy access to it.

We started this organization during the pandemic when many of our sports were cancelled. We had to overcome many setbacks and delays because of the pandemic but we succeeded in opening up our organization. We have children from various areas join each event and look forward to growing.
