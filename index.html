Props for Peds

Props for Peds is a fundraiser launched by two siblings, Nicholas and Mary Gioules. Nicholas is 17 and Mary is 15, and they both have put in lots of time to coordinate this fundraiser. Nicholas and Mary have been dedicated for most of their lives to helping the disabled and lending care to those who need. Since a young age, they've been taught to treat people with the utmost respect and give their help to those who may be in more of a need for it. Helping young kids with disabilities or disorders has been a passion of each of theirs ever since little children, and to further aid to these children, he's created a fundraiser which has gave him the ability to do so.
